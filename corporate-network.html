How We Got Here

The blockchain trail (Page 2) established that the deptoy.co fake exchange was operated under the corporate name BISSNEX CRYPTO GROUP LIMITED (Canada). Searching that company name led to its previous names (BIPPAX, ALPHA TECHNOLOGY), its FINTRAC registration, and the broader network of domains associated with the BIPPAX platform. Those domains — byxgcoins.com, byxgexchange.com, bippaxcoins.com — led to Colorado Secretary of State records, where the same registered agent name appeared repeatedly: Darryl Joel Dorfman.

Fake Press Releases (Wire Fraud)
Three press releases were submitted to GlobeNewswire in December 2023 containing materially false statements:
  • "BISSNEX at the Forefront of Global Crypto Industry Compliance" (2023-12-28)
  • "BISSNEX Plans to Implement More Measures for Global Compliant Operations" (2023-12-28)
  • "Defying the Odds: BISSNEX's Journey Through a Bear Market to a Bright Future" (2023-12-27)
These falsely claimed BISSNEX received funding from Paradigm, NGC Ventures, Multicoin Capital, and Pantera Capital — none of which funded BISSNEX. These false claims constitute wire fraud (18 USC §1343) and provide a US-nexus ground for subpoena of GlobeNewswire account records.

Darryl Joel Dorfman — The Western Wrapper

  1. BYXG Cryptocurrency Group Ltd (CO #20231159680) — registered by DARRYL JOEL DORFMAN. BYXG domains (byxgcoins.com, byxgexchange.com) are listed as BIPPAX-network scam platforms on reportyourscam.com.

Caveat: "Registered agent" does not automatically equal "operator." Dorfman could be a nominee. However, the BaFin/Nanolite link and his direct WhatsApp persona are stronger evidence of active participation.

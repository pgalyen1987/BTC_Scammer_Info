Operation Overview

Pig-butchering (sha zhu pan, 杀猪盘) is a long-horizon social engineering fraud in which victims are cultivated over weeks or months through romantic or investment relationships before being persuaded to deposit funds into a fake exchange platform — the "slaughter." Once a sufficient balance has accumulated the platform blocks withdrawals and the operators vanish. The name refers to fattening a pig before killing it.

All three operations documented in this investigation use technology of Chinese origin: the ThinkPHP PHP framework, aaPanel (BaoTa) Chinese server management software, and/or commercial pig-butchering-as-a-service kits sourced from Chinese code-sharing platforms and vendors. This is consistent with the broader Southeast Asian compound ecosystem documented by The Record (March 2026), in which criminal syndicates — many operating from fortified compounds in Myanmar, Cambodia, and the Philippines — license Chinese-developed fraud software to run industrialised fraud operations against victims worldwide.

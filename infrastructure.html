All the domains observed on the Hong Kong server, the Japanese typosquats, and the Malaysian impersonation sites share one fingerprint: they use nameservers under SHARE-DNS.COM and SHARE-DNS.NET. Both SHARE-DNS domains and mdutton.com (DUTT Exchange) are registered at the same registrar, Gname.com. Whether they share the same Gname account is unconfirmed — registrant details are privacy-redacted in WHOIS. A subpoena to Gname is required to establish account-level linkage.

A. ICP LOOKUP — DO THIS YOURSELF (CAPTCHA REQUIRED)
================================================================================

The Chinese MIIT ICP database is publicly accessible but requires a visual
CAPTCHA. This will reveal the company name (主办单位) and possibly the legal
representative behind 沪ICP备13026899号-3.

STEP 1: Go to: https://beian.miit.gov.cn/
STEP 2: Click "备案信息查询" (Record Information Query) in the navigation
STEP 3: In the search box, select "备案号" (Registration Number) as search type
STEP 4: Enter: 13026899
STEP 5: Complete the slider/image CAPTCHA
STEP 6: Record the following fields from the result:
  - 主办单位名称 (Organizer/Company name)
  - 主办单位性质 (Entity type: enterprise/individual)
  - 网站名称 (Website name)
  - 域名 (Domain name)
  - 备案/许可证号 (Full registration number with province prefix)

ALTERNATIVE (easier CAPTCHA):
  Go to: https://icp.chinaz.com/
  Enter: 沪ICP备13026899号-3
  Click search — may have simpler verification

WHAT THIS WILL LIKELY SHOW:
  The entity that registered this ICP number. Under Chinese law (互联网信息服务管理办法),
  the registrant must provide their real company name and legal representative.
  If registered as an individual (个人), the registrant's real name and ID are on file
  with MIIT and the hosting provider.

  NOTE: The public page shows company name + domain only. The full application
  (including legal representative name, ID number, phone) is held by MIIT and
  the hosting provider (Alibaba Cloud, Tencent Cloud, etc.) and requires
  official law enforcement request to obtain.
